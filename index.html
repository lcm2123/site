Skills

I'm a User Experience Designer and Strategist.

I specialize in the design of information: from designing user flows and structuring information hierarchies—to information design and infographics—to an inherent comfort with working in databases, I’ve always relished the opportunity to make information accessible.

With a backgroung in Architecture, I apply my understanding of spatial experiences to the realm of information experiences, and I am passionate about structure. With over 5 years as a graphic designer, I bring an enthusiasm for systems of visual hierarchy and layout. Finally, with experience in project management and brand design strategy, as well as HTML and CSS coding skills, I can effectively commmunicate as part of cross-disciplinary teams to build great experiences.
